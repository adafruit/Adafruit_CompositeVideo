Detailed Description

DMA-driven composite video library for M0 microcontrollers (Circuit Playground Express, Feather M0, Arduino Zero, etc.). Gator-clip composite video 'tip' to pin A0, 'ring' to GND. Handles grayscale NTSC video, 40x24 pixels, usable area may be smaller due to overscan.
